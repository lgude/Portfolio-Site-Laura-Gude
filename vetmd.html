PROJECT OVERVIEW

Context & Role

VetMD is a student project I completed for CareerFoundry. The task was to design a mobile-first application, and my role was UX/UI and brand designer.

Design Problem

Design and create an app that helps pet owners connect with veterinarians virtually. Create a cohesive brand and design throughout the whole app.

Duration

3 months total

COLOR SCHEME

Finding a veterinarian isn’t a fun process but it’s something every pet owner has to deal with, however that doesn’t mean it has to be miserable! Colors were chosen to make the app seem exciting and fun for a user to use. I want the user to enjoy using our app so therefore the colors need to add to that.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Our pet owner users need a way to connect with licensed veterinarians quickly because they have questions about their pets and/or need a diagnosis for an ailment without having to wait a long time to see a veterinarian in person.
We will know this statement to be true when we see an increase in the number of pet owners using this online service and we receive positive reviews and feedback for the service.

USABILITY TESTING

Goals
The goal of this study is to access the learnability and efficiency of navigating the application for the first time by finding out how to do three of the key functions - find a vet, message a vet and order a medication, using the mobile application. I would also like to understand any room for improvement through the thoughts and feelings of the test subjects.

Test Objectives

  • Determine if the participants understand what the application’s purpose is quickly.
  • Determine if the navigational bar makes sense to the participants.
  • Determine if the participants can quickly and easily find a veterinarian’s profile.
  • Determine if the participants can figure out two different ways to message a veterinarian.
  • Determine if the participants find it easy to order a prescribed medication.
  • Determine how easily the participants can navigate through the mobile app.
  • Find any design or functional problems with the mobile application.

FEATURES

After using testing and interviews, I realized the four most important key features for this app.

Quick and easy onboarding

The user will be taken through a sign-up that includes pet information and owner information. The user will also have to chose the plan they want for the app to proceed. The user than goes through an onboarding process that takes them through the different features of the app. They will also have the ability to skip through this if they want.

Find a Vet

Users will be able to search for vets through the homepage or they can filter and search through the appointments page to narrow down the search to find the perfect vet for them!

Video Chat & Messaging

A messaging feature that allows pet owners to ask veterinarians quick questions. Users are also allowed to make video calls with veterinarians for more in-depth questions and exams all while the owner and pet are comfortable in their own home!

Buy Products

From medication to toys and everything in between, users will be able to buy them all for their furry loved ones. Users will no longer have to jump through hoops to get simple medication for their pets and it will be conveniently shipped to their home.
