furrever homes

Online Pet Adoption App | UX/UI Mobile Design

Furrever homes was an app designed to help connect prospective pet owners with loving animals in an easy and manageable way. It also allows people to post animals that are available for adoption, creating an all-in-one app to find animals their furrever home.

Project Overview

Context & Role

The concept was brought up to me by my sister who is having a difficult time trying to find a puppy for her family. I was the UX/UI and Brand designer for the app.

Design Problem

Create an app that helps prospective pet owners find pets easier and more reliably. Create a cohesive brand and design that carries throughout the whole app.

Duration

3 weeks total

Pain Points

Finding a pet is time-consuming

Sorting through several different places from shelters, newspaper clippings and Craigslist can be exhausting and time-consuming.

Adopting can often be unreliable

There are a lot of scams out there so there needs to be an app that people can trust 100%.

Not enough information on animals

Most pet adoption sites often only include very basic information about pets so you really don’t get a feel for the animal.

Color Scheme

Colors were chosen to make the app seem fun and inviting for people to look through adoptable pets. This is a fun and exciting process and that’s what I wanted it to come across while you’re scrolling to find your new family member!

Userflow

Features

After interviewing potential users, I came up with four key features that most users were looking for.

Quick and easy onboarding

After signing up, the user will be taken to a three page onboarding to show them how the app works. This helps the user get a clear picture of what to expect right from the beginning.

Find a Pet

The filter buttons make finding the right animal for the user quick and easy. The user will have the option to delete the filter choices without having to go back and fill out all filter options again, creating less frustration.

Adopt a Pet

The application is thorough without being too overwhelming and tedious for the user. After completing the application, the app will keep the user updated on where they are at in the adoption process so they will never feel in the dark about what to do.

Put a Pet up for Adoption

The process of putting an animal up for adoption is quick and efficient but also thorough enough so a user can get enough information about the animal to adopt them. This creates a way for people to have a reliable way to get animals adopted!
